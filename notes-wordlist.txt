# Word List Woes
Will select words that are of length 3-6

## /usr/share/dict/words: 104334 lines
Problems:
* Has plural of nouns
* Has possessives of nouns
* Has present participles of verbs (seeing, reading, thinking, cursing)
* Has acronyms (always UPPPER CASE)
* Has names (always Title case)
Examples:
zygote
zygote's
zygotes
ACLU
Aimee

Tactic:
- Filter possessives with grep -v "'".
- Filter acronyms by detection of upper case.
- Filter names by title case.
